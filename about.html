About Us

The World's leading reusable toothpick supplier

Acoording to some unverified report made about a scientific study not referenced anywhere, up to 450 billion toothpicks are getting used once per lifecycle, and discarded subsequently.

EcoToothpick solves this environmental and social issue by producing high quality reusable toothpicks for any occasion.

The company was founded in a post-WW2 Zagreb, having main headquarters and primary R&D facility on-site. All of the factories are located within 12.000 kilometers from the US capital city.
